About Me

Creative Graphic Designer

I am Amena Rasooli, a Graphic Designer with over two years of experience in creating logos, brand identities, posters, brochures, and visual content.

Before starting my journey in graphic design, I studied Dentistry in Iran. Over time, I discovered my true passion for creativity and visual storytelling, which led me to shift my career toward design.

My goal is to create meaningful, clean, and professional designs that help brands communicate effectively and stand out visually.
